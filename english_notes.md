# 英语面试复习笔记 (西北大学MBA复试专用 - 精确版)

## **Topic 1: 自我介绍 (Self-Introduction)**

**目标：在90秒内，将你从一个“技术执行者”塑造成一个有“商业思维和管理潜力”的申请人。**

#### **你的专属范文 (MBA定制版)**
> "Good morning, professors. It is a great honor to be here. My name is Zhang Shengping.
>
> For the past few years, I've been working as a Back-end Engineer at **Tencent Cloud**, focusing on building powerful cloud services.
>
> Most recently, I was a core developer for an exciting project called '**AI Talk**'. In simple terms, our service helps businesses create smart voice assistants. My job was to build the '**brain**' of this system. By making the system fast and reliable, we helped our clients **significantly reduce customer waiting times and improve their service quality**.
>
> This project was a **turning point** for me. I realized that I enjoy not only building the technology, but also thinking about the **strategy** behind it: *What* problems should we solve? And *how* can our technology create more business value?
>
> My career goal is to transition from a technical expert into a leader in the tech industry, for example, as a **Product Manager**.
>
> I believe Northwestern University’s MBA, with its strong focus on **strategy and leadership**, is the perfect next step for me to gain the business knowledge I need to achieve this goal. Thank you."

*   **练习技巧**: **不要死记硬背！** 记住**加粗的关键词**（Tencent Cloud, AI Talk, build the brain, reduce waiting times, turning point, strategy, Product Manager），用自己的话自然地串联起来。

## **Topic 2: 其他常见问题框架**

*   **你的工作职责 (Your Job/Responsibilities)?**
    *   直接使用上面范文的“AI Talk”部分，可以稍微扩展一下技术挑战或团队合作的细节。
*   **你的优缺点 (Your Strengths/Weaknesses)?**
    *   **优点**: 结合你的工作说。例如: "My strength is my problem-solving ability. As an engineer, I am trained to break down complex problems and find logical solutions."
    *   **缺点**: 说一个真实、无伤大雅、且正在改进的缺点。例如: "Sometimes I might focus too much on technical details and lose sight of the bigger picture. That's exactly why I want to pursue an MBA, to strengthen my strategic thinking."
*   **你的职业规划 (Your Career Plan)?**
    *   短期(Short-term, 3-5 years): Become a Product Manager in a tech company.
    *   长期(Long-term, 5-10 years): Become a leader in the tech industry, for example, a Director of Product, and lead a team to create innovative products.
